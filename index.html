CS 1300 Persona and Storyboards

This is the water dispenser in the CIT. It is meant to dispense water in a contact-free fashion to combat covid, and the user is meant to hover over the actual buttons themselves to eliminate the need to touch a public surface. Note that if the user pushes on the buttons instead of hovering, no water will come out.

The above is a sketch of the interface. Key components include: the dispense sensor, the hot water sensor, and the various icons showing the status of the dispenser.

Part 1 - Observations and Interviews

Key Observations

I observed 4 people in total using the dispenser. Most of the users I observed knew how to use the water dispenser. They used the dispenser by hovering their hands over the dispense button, and one of them did know how to get hot water. There was one user who was using it for the first time. He spent some time reading the paper sign on the dispenser before hovering his hand over the dispense button.
